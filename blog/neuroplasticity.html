Neuroplasticity & Growth Mindset

Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity is the brain’s ability to change and adapt. The basic principles have many benefits, from learning new things, adapting to change. If we focus on a task and we are motivated and engaged to get it done our brains release neurochemicals but if we are the opposite unmotivated and unengaged our brains don’t release neurochemicals. But at the end of the day focusing on our brain health is one of the best things we can do for ourselves to improve concentration, focus, memory, and mental agility. If we incorporate brain exercises into our everyday lives and challenge our minds, we will sharpen our skills and have a much happier enriching life.

Ways to increase our neuroplasticity is doing things like complicated puzzles to get your brain working, research even shows playing videos games can increase our neuroplastic from reaction time, coordination, problem-solving. Even learning a new language can help with neuroplasticity, helps with attention, emotions, memory, and motor skills.
