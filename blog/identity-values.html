No Manutuke Ahou

Ko Terry Jo Blyth ahou

Kia Ora, my name is Terry Jo Blyth I am from Manutuke on the East Coast of Gisborne, growing up I went to Manutuke School where I learnt Te Reo Maori, which was my first language growing up, I also did Kapa Haka winning reginals numerious times and winning the New Zealand Primary School Kapa Haka Competetion, my culture taught me alot not only about myself but about this Aorangi (Universe). Maori Values like "Manaakitanga" looking out for others, "Whanaungatanga" the importance of building and fostering relationships, "Kaitiakitanga" looking after the environment and everything for future generations. "Kotahitanga" unity working together as one, "Maramatanga" Understanding & Enlightenment, Core Values that I follow everyday, without my culture I wouldn't be the person I am today. in
