My Learning Plan

What is your long-term goal and/or career pathway?

Honestly, my long term goal is just to get good at what I do and get a good job. Coming from a small place like Gisborne, there's not many opportunities here and I want to experience much more and I feel like the I.T industry can help with that.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning.

I am a very motivated person, when I wanna do something I won’t stop until it is done, I like to work hard and keep focused. Sometimes I can doubt myself and think I can’t do it, I just need to stay positive and believe in myself.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

My social skills to learn to keep conversations going, talking in large groups. My confidence in being able to believe in myself.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this program

I have written a weekly plan that I want to try and keep sticking to, studying at least 6-8 hours a day and a few hours at night, I make sure I take enough breaks during the day so I’m staying grounded, every morning I like to do a 15-minute meditation when I get up, to relax my mind and to set my intentions for the day. I am fully committed to this course. I am happy to learn by myself and within a group, I like to get along with others and make sure everyone feels included, and also to help others when they need help, I like to treat this as a job and be professional in everything I do, staying in contact with students or the facilitator and sticking to deadlines. If I don’t know what I’m doing I am not afraid to ask questions or ask for help. If I feel myself feeling blocked it’s good to just take a break and meditate, release all the tension.
